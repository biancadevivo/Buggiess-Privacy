Privacy Policy – Buggies

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Buggies is an educational application designed to help children aged 9–11 build familiarity with insects through storytelling, interactive tasks, and augmented reality experiences.

We respect user privacy and designed the app so that no personal data is collected or stored.

Information We Collect

Buggies does not collect personal information.

  • No account creation is required
  • No personal identifiers are collected
  • No user tracking is performed
  • No analytics services are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

All interactions occur locally on the user's device.

Camera Usage

Buggies uses the device camera to enable Augmented Reality (AR) features.

The camera allows children to view virtual insects placed in their real-world environment.

No images, videos, or camera data are recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Microphone Usage

The app uses the device microphone for a specific interactive activity where children can blow into the microphone to interact with elements in the app.

The microphone is used only to detect the blowing action required for the activity.

No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted. All processing happens locally on the device.

Children's Privacy

Buggies is designed as an educational experience for children.

  • No personal data is collected from children
  • No advertisements are displayed
  • No in-app purchases are included
  • No behavior tracking is performed

The app is designed to provide a safe and privacy-respecting learning environment.

Third-Party Services

Buggies does not integrate advertising networks, analytics tools, or third-party data collection services.

The application uses Apple frameworks including:

  • ARKit
  • RealityKit
  • AVFoundation

These technologies operate locally on the device.

Buggies – Explore Insects with Fun and Safety!

About Buggies

Buggies is a free educational app designed for children aged 9–11 to explore the fascinating world of insects through storytelling, interactive activities, and augmented reality experiences.

Key Features

  • Interactive storytelling to engage curiosity
  • Augmented Reality (AR) to view insects in your environment
  • Safe and private: no personal data collected
  • Fun activities like “blow to interact” games
  • Completely free with no ads or in-app purchases

Privacy & Safety

Buggies prioritizes children's privacy and safety:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No tracking or analytics is used
  • All interactions happen locally on the device
  • No advertisements or in-app purchases
  • Camera and microphone are only used locally for AR and interaction

Educational Benefits

  • Encourages curiosity about nature and insects
  • Develops observation and critical thinking skills
  • Supports safe digital exploration for children
  • Integrates science learning with interactive play

Supported Technologies

Buggiess uses Apple frameworks to provide a smooth AR and interactive experience:

  • ARKit – for augmented reality features
  • RealityKit – for realistic 3D rendering
  • AVFoundation – for safe interactive audio actions

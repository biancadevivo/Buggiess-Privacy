Buggiess – Explore Insects with Fun and Safety!

Discover Buggiess

Buggiess is a free educational app for children aged 9–11. Explore the fascinating world of insects through storytelling, interactive activities, and augmented reality.

Why Parents Love It

  • 100% safe: no personal data is collected
  • No ads and no in-app purchases
  • Engaging AR experiences to spark curiosity
  • Interactive games that teach while kids play
  • Works completely offline, all processing happens on the device

Educational Benefits

  • Encourages curiosity about nature
  • Supports observation and critical thinking skills
  • Combines science learning with fun and play
  • Promotes safe digital exploration for children

Technology

Buggiess uses Apple frameworks to provide smooth AR and interactive experiences:

  • ARKit – realistic augmented reality
  • RealityKit – 3D rendering of insects
  • AVFoundation – safe interactive audio features
